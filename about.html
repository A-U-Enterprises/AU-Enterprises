WHO ARE WE

01

Experienced Team with a Customer-Centric Approach

At AU Enterprises, we are a team of passionate and experienced professionals committed to delivering genuine and cost-effective solutions. Our main goal is to support customers with OEM-standard replacement and repair parts, ensuring seamless operations for the crushing industry.

02

Top-Quality Parts and Components for Crushing Industrieszz

Since 2012, we have specialized in providing high-quality parts and components tailored to the crushing industry. Our extensive product range includes jaw plates, cone liners, impact bars, and more. Each item is rigorously tested to meet industry standards for performance, durability, and value.

03

Exceptional Customer Service and Technical Support

We pride ourselves on offering outstanding customer service and technical support. Our knowledgeable team works closely with clients to ensure they receive the right parts when they need them. With a reliable supply chain and deep industry expertise, AU Enterprises is a trusted partner for businesses in the crushing sector.
